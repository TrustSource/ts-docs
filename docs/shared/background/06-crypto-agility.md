# Crypto Agility

Crypto agility is the ability to quickly switch cryptographic algorithms and protocols without major redesigns — essential as algorithms become deprecated or broken.

## Why it matters

- Post-quantum computing threatens current asymmetric algorithms (RSA, ECC).
- Regulatory requirements increasingly mandate crypto inventories.
- Export controls classify software based on encryption capabilities.
