# Legal Manager

> [!NOTE]
> **Required role:** `legal_manager`

This menu group is visible only to users with the **legal_manager** role. It provides dedicated views for managing legal aspects of open-source compliance — license curation, legal templates and trademark/copyright tracking.

| Page | What it covers |
|---|---|
| [Manage Legal](01-manage-legal.md) | Curate license interpretations, review and edit license details. |
| [Legal Templates](02-legal-templates.md) | Obligation templates and notice file templates for compliance documents. |
| [Copyright & Trademarks](03-copyright-trademarks.md) | Company-wide trademark and copyright registry. |
