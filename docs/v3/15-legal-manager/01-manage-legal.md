# Manage Legal

> [!NOTE]
> **Required role:** `legal_manager`

The legal management view lets you curate license interpretations across the company portfolio. Where the standard license database provides general information, this view lets legal managers add company-specific assessments, override default obligation mappings and maintain a reviewed-license inventory.

## What you can do

- **Review licenses** found in scans and mark them as reviewed, approved or restricted for your company context.
- **Edit license details** — add company-specific notes, interpretation guidance, or override default obligation flags.
- **Create private licenses** for proprietary or custom license texts not in the public database.
- **Set commercial terms** — define whether and under what conditions a license is acceptable for your use cases.

📸 *Screenshot: the Manage Legal list showing licenses with review status, company notes and action buttons.*

## Difference from Knowledge → Licenses

The [Knowledge → Licenses](../05-knowledge/06-licenses.md) page shows the public license database and is readable by all users. This management page adds the ability to curate, annotate and override interpretations for your company.
