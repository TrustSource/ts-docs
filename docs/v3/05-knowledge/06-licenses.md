# Licenses

The license database contains detailed information about open-source and commercial licenses — obligations, permissions, restrictions, compatibility notes and your company's approval status.

## What you can do

- **Search** by license name or SPDX identifier.
- **View obligations** — attribution, copyleft, patent grant, source disclosure, etc.
- **See commercial terms** — if configured for your company.
- **Check approval status** — allowed, restricted or forbidden per your policy.
