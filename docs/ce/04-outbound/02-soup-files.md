# SOUP Files (Medical)

> [!NOTE]
> **Required role:** `compliance_manager`, `manager` or `account_admin`
> **Required license feature:** `medical` + `soup_report`

SOUP (Software of Unknown Provenance) files document third-party software components in medical device projects, as required by **IEC 62304** and **MDR/IVDR** regulations.

## What you can do

- **Generate** a SOUP list from a module's component data.
- **Map components** to SOUP entries with classification and risk assessment.
- **Version and freeze** SOUP files at approval time.
