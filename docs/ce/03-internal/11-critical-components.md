# Critical Components

> [!NOTE]
> **Required role:** `company_component_manager`

The Critical Components view shows components flagged as high-risk across your company portfolio. A component may be critical because of:

- **High vulnerability count** — many open CVEs.
- **Wide usage** — used in many modules across the company.
- **End-of-life** — approaching or past its support end date.
- **License risk** — uses a restricted or forbidden license.

## What you can do

- Review the list of critical components sorted by risk.
- Drill into a component to see which modules use it.
- Prioritize remediation based on usage breadth and severity.
