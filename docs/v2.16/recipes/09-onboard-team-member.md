# Recipe: Onboard a Team Member

Invite a new user and get them productive.

## Steps

1. Go to [Administration → User Management](../06-administration/04-user-management.md).
2. Click **Invite** and enter the new user's email.
3. Assign appropriate [roles](../10-roles-permissions/01-roles-overview.md).
4. The user receives an enrollment email and sets up their account.
5. Share links to [Getting Started](../00-getting-started/index.md) and the [5-Minute Tour](../00-getting-started/04-quick-tour.md).
