# Recipe: Share an SBOM Publicly

Share an SBOM with a customer without giving them a TrustSource login.

## Steps

1. Approve the module via the [approval workflow](../03-internal/06-approvals/index.md).
2. Mark the approval as [public](../03-internal/06-approvals/05-public-approves.md).
3. Share the public URL with your customer.
4. The customer can access the SBOM, Notice file and (if generated) SOUP list.
