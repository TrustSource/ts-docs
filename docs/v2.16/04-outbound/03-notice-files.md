# Notice Files

> [!NOTE]
> **Required role:** `compliance_manager`, `manager` or `account_admin`

Notice files (also called attribution files) document the open-source components used in your software and their license obligations. They are the file you ship with your product to comply with attribution requirements.

## What you can do

- **Generate** a notice file from a module's component and license data.
- **Edit** the generated content — add custom notices, adjust formatting.
- **Version and freeze** at approval time.
- **Download** as text or PDF.
- **Share publicly** via a public URL (see [Public Approves](../03-internal/06-approvals/05-public-approves.md)).
