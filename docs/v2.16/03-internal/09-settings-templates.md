# Settings & Templates (Internal Context)

This page is a cross-reference to the company-wide template and settings management in the [Administration chapter](../06-administration/09-templates.md).

Within the Internal area, you encounter templates when:

- Importing **legal templates** into project or module settings.
- Using **private license** definitions created by your admin.
- Applying **private algorithm** configurations.
- Working with **tag** definitions for projects and modules.

All these templates are managed centrally under **Administration → Templates**. See [Templates](../06-administration/09-templates.md) for the full reference.
